Frequently Asked Questions

Is the program free? +
PC Check is available in a free version that provides basic scanning and hack detection functions, in addition to a paid (Premium) version that provides comprehensive protection and additional features such as real-time protection and excellent technical support.
Is the program compatible with all operating systems? +
Currently, PC Check supports all versions of Windows operating system (7/8/10/11). We are working on providing versions compatible with MacOS and Linux in the near future.
What are the system requirements? +
The basic requirements are: Windows 7 or newer operating system, processor speed of at least 1.5 GHz, RAM of 2 GB or more, and 500 MB of hard disk space.
How can I get technical support? +
You can get technical support through: Email (support@pccheck.com), live chat on our website (available 24/7), or through the dedicated phone number for paid version users.
How often should I scan my device? +
We recommend performing a comprehensive scan at least once a week. If you use the internet extensively or download various files, you may need to scan more frequently. In the paid version, partial scanning operations are performed automatically on a regular basis.
